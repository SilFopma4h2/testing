Partnership Network

The Ocean Trust Organization

Partnership Type: Coral Restoration Collaboration

Focus Areas: Marine Conservation, Community Engagement, Skills Transfer

Location: Kenya - Pate Island and surrounding coastal areas

Partnership Impact

We are honored to work alongside The Ocean Trust Organization in a joint effort to restore coral reefs in Kenya. This partnership ensures that restoration is community-driven, with local people at the heart of every activity.

Coral Restoration Partnership

In partnership with The Ocean Trust Organization, our community has taken an active role in coral reef restoration efforts in the coastal waters of Kenya.

Through this collaboration:

  • Local community members are directly engaged in restoration activities
  • Skills, knowledge, and tools are shared to empower people in marine conservation
  • Together, we are helping rebuild vibrant coral ecosystems that support biodiversity and livelihoods
  • Capacity building programs for sustainable marine resource management
