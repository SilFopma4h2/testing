Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Patte Conservation.

How can I volunteer remotely?

We offer many remote volunteer opportunities including marketing, graphic design, web development, content writing, research, and administrative support. Contact us with your skills and availability.

Do you accept in-kind donations?

Yes, we accept relevant in-kind donations such as medical supplies, educational materials, and technology equipment. Please contact us before sending any items to ensure they meet current needs.

Can I visit project locations?

We welcome visitors to our project locations, but visits must be coordinated in advance for safety and logistics. Contact our regional offices to arrange educational visits or volunteer trips.

How do I stay updated on your work?

Subscribe to our newsletter, follow us on social media, or check our website regularly for project updates, impact reports, and news about our work around the world.
