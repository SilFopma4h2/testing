Our Mission

To empower the Patte community to sustainably manage and conserve their marine resources through the establishment and effective management of community conservation areas, improving livelihood, safeguarding biodiversity, and enhancing the overall well-being of the community.

Coral Restoration

Active restoration of coral reefs in Kenya through community-led initiatives and partnerships with marine conservation organizations.

Eco-Tourism

Sustainable marine tourism including snorkeling, diving, and sea adventures that support local livelihoods.

Marine Conservation

Establishing Locally Managed Marine Areas (LMMA) to preserve biodiversity and ensure sustainable fishing practices.
